2015 Pagani Huayra

The Pagani Huayra is a mid-engine super car produced by Italian super car manufacturer Pagani
The word Huayra came from a Quechua which translates to the word for wind.

The 2015 Pagani Huayra is a high-end supercar with a luxurious and sophisticated interior.

The interior features a combination of carbon fiber, leather, and aluminum, as well as advanced technology and craftsmanship. The front seats are comfortable with a sporty design and adjustable side bolsters. They are upholstered in a combination of leather and Alcantara, with contrasting stitching and Pagani branding.

The dashboard is sleek and uncluttered, with a 9-inch infotainment display. The infotainment system features a variety of connectivity options. It is controlled by a rotary dial and a set of buttons on the center console, as well as by a touch screen and a set of steering wheel controls.

The 2015 Pagani Huayra is powered by a Mercedes-AMG M158 6.0-liter V12 engine.

This engine is capable of producing 720 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 7-speed automated manual transmission and a rear-wheel drive system. The Huayra has a top speed of about 238 mph and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in about 3.0 seconds.

The M158 V12 engine was developed specifically for the Huayra and is notable for its high power output, low weight, and impressive fuel efficiency.
