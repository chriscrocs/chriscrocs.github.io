The Rolls-Royce Wraith is a well known car in terms of luxury, Founded and Manufactured in the United Kingdom
The Spirit of Ecstasy is the bonnet ornament sculpture on Rolls-Royce cars. It is in the form of a woman leaning

The interior of the 2021 Rolls-Royce Wraith is designed to be a luxurious and comfortable space for passengers.

It features high-quality materials such as leather and wood, and is finished with attention to detail and craftsmanship. The seats are spacious and feature power adjustability and heating and cooling functions.

It is also equipped with a range of advanced technology features, including a touchscreen system, a premium sound system, and a heads-up display that projects important information. There are also a number of convenience features, such as a panoramic sunroof and a refrigerated compartment in the center console for storing drinks.

The 2021 Rolls-Royce Wraith is powered by a 6.6-liter V12 engine that produces 624 horsepower and 642 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and is capable of propelling the Wraith from 0 to 60 mph in just 4.4 seconds.

The V12 engine is known for its smooth and powerful performance. The Wraith is also equipped with all-wheel drive for improved traction and stability in a variety of road conditions. It is also designed to be fuel efficient, thanks to its advanced technology and lightweight construction. It has an EPA-estimated fuel economy rating of 13 mpg in the city and 21 mpg on the highway.
