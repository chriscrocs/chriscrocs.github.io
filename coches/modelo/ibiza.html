2018 Seat Ibiza

The SEAT Ibiza has a Spanish bloodline. Founded and manufactured in Spain the car was named after
the Spanish island of Ibiza. The SEAT Ibiza is also known for being reliable and an economic car.

The interior of the Seat Ibiza 2018 model is designed with style and functionality in mind.

It features a modern dashboard with a sleek layout, as well as comfortable and supportive seats. to enhance the driving experience, such as a touchscreen infotainment system, a digital instrument cluster, and a variety of connectivity options such as Bluetooth and USB ports.

The Seat Ibiza is a compact & economic car that has been in production since 1984.

Here is a list of some of the engine options that were available for the 2018 Seat Ibiza:

  • 1.0-liter TSI petrol engine with 95 horsepower
  • 1.0-liter TSI petrol engine with 115 horsepower
  • 1.5-liter TSI petrol engine with 150 horsepower
  • 1.6-liter TDI diesel engine with 95 horsepower
  • 1.6-liter TDI diesel engine with 115 horsepower

    • These engines are available with either a manual or automatic transmission.
