2019 Dodge RAM

The Dodge RAM is a full-size American pickup truck that started it's production since the year 1980's
The Ram trucks have been named Motor Trend magazine's Truck of the Year eight times

The 2019 Dodge Ram 1500 is a full-size pickup truck that offers a spacious and comfortable interior.

The 2019 Dodge Ram 1500 comes with a six-speaker audio system, a 8.4-inch touchscreen display, a nine-speaker Alpine audio system, and a premium leather-wrapped steering wheel. In terms of comfort and convenience, it features front bucket seats, as well as a power-adjustable driver's seat. The truck also has a spacious rear seat with plenty of legroom and headroom for passengers.

The 2019 Dodge Ram 1500 is available with a variety of engine options.

The base engine is a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 305 horsepower and 269 pound-feet of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. In addition to the base V6 engine, it is also available with a 5.7-liter V8 engine that produces 395 horsepower and 410 pound-feet of torque.

The 2019 Dodge Ram 1500 is also available with a 5.7-liter V8 engine that has been modified with the Mopar Performance Parts package. This engine produces an impressive 420 horsepower and 460 pound-feet of torque, making it one of the most powerful engines available in a full-size pickup truck.
